About Us

DD Ride Service LLC was founded with a mission to revolutionize the ride-sharing industry. In recent years, drivers have faced increasing challenges and exploitation from major ride-share and gig economy companies. These challenges include unsustainable pay, lack of transparency, and unfair treatment. We started DD Ride Service to create a fairer, more transparent, and sustainable option for both drivers and customers.
